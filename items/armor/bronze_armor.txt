====== Bronze Armor ======
----
Armor crafted from <mcitem>techreborn:bronze_ingot</mcitem>.

When fully equipped, you'll get **16 Defence points** and **+4 Knockback Resistance**.

===== Usage =====
----
Wear to gain a protective effect. A helmet, chestplate, leggings, and boots are equipped by placing them in the head, chest, legs and  feet slots of the inventory next to the player's character, respectively. You can also right-click to equip armor. If the slot is occupied, the items will swap places.
