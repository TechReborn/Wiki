====== Block of Aluminium =======
//This article is about the mineral block; see the following articles about similar topics: <mcitem>techreborn:Aluminium_Ingot</mcitem>, <mcitem>techreborn:Aluminium_Nugget</mcitem>, <mcitem>techreborn:Small_Pile_of_Aluminium_Dust</mcitem>, <mcitem>techreborn:Aluminium_Plate</mcitem>.//

A **block of aluminium** is a precious metal block equivalent to nine <mcitem>techreborn:aluminium_ingot</mcitem>s.

===== Obtaining =====
==== Breaking ====
Blocks of aluminium can be mined with any or no tool.
