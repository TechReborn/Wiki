**Quantum Armor** is a top tier armor set provided by Tech Reborn. 
Helm gives you water breathing, chest piece provides creative flight and extinguish fire, leggings increases your movement speed and feet increases your swimming speed. 
Besides that each piece absorb 20% of incoming damage at the cost of energy with total absorption value of 80% for a full set. Damage absorption done after damage reduction due to defense and before any damage reduction done by enchants.

Defense itself also boosted to 20 when armor piece has energy charged. Same applies for knockback resistance.

## Usage

Wear it.

When all quantum equipment is equipped, the set effect will be triggered and the player attributes will be added.

## Key Binds

Press **N** Toggle Night Vision

Press **R** Toggle Sprint Speed

**Active** will be displayed in the item description when switching.
