**Omni-Tool** is a universal tool that combines Pickaxe, Axe, Shovel, Shears, and even Sword in one simple rod. It has 1M energy storage. One can charge it using <McItem slug="techreborn:battery_box" inline={true}/>. Its mining level is on par with diamond tools. One mined block will cost 100 Energy and it will use 125 Energy on entity hit.
