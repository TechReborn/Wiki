**Rock Cutter** is a Diamond like tool with Silk Touch enchantment. It has 10k Energy storage. It uses Energy instead of durability to break a block. It will cost 10 Energy to break one block.

## Usage

Main usage for **Rock Cutter** is to produce <McItem slug="minecraft:stone" inline={true}/> instead of <McItem slug="minecraft:cobblestone" inline={true}/> during your mining sessions. Also it will produce ore blocks for those who are used to modded ore doubling.
