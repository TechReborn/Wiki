Refined Iron Ingot

A Refined Iron Ingot is used in many Tech Reborn recipes. It is typically acquired by smelting

Iron Ingots in any kind of furnace, such as a
Furnace,
Blast Furnace, Iron Furnace or Electric Furnace.
