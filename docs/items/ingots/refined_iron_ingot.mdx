# Refined Iron Ingot

A **Refined Iron Ingot** is used in many Tech Reborn recipes. It is typically acquired by smelting <McItem slug="minecraft:iron_ingot"/>s in any kind of furnace, such as a <McItem slug="minecraft:furnace"/>, <McItem slug="minecraft:blast_furnace"/>, <McItem slug="techreborn:iron_furnace"/> or <McItem slug="techreborn:electric_furnace"/>.
