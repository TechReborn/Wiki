# Refined Iron Ingot

A **Refined Iron Ingot** is used in many Tech Reborn recipes. It is typically acquired by is smelting <McItem slug="minecraft:iron_ingot" inline={true}/>s in any kind of furnace, such as a <McItem slug="minecraft:furnace" inline={true}/>, <McItem slug="minecraft:blast_furnace" inline={true}/>, <McItem slug="techreborn:iron_furnace" inline={true}/> or <McItem slug="techreborn:electric_furnace" inline={true}/>.
