The **Nitro Diesel Cell** is a fluid cell added by Tech Reborn. It is used to generate energy in <McItem slug="techreborn:diesel_generator" inline={true}/>.

## Recipe

\<mcitem\>techreborn:chemical_reactor\</mcitem\> recipe:\
\<mcitem\>techreborn:diesel_cell\</mcitem\> + \<mcitem\>techreborn:glyceryl_cell\</mcitem\> -\> 5x\<mcitem\>techreborn:nitrodiesel_cell\</mcitem\>

## Usage

<McItem slug="techreborn:nitrodiesel_cell" inline={true}/> is used to generate energy. 1 cell or bucket will generate 400k Energy in <McItem slug="techreborn:diesel_generator" inline={true}/>.
