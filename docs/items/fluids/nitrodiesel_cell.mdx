The **Nitro Diesel Cell** is a fluid cell added by Tech Reborn. It is used to generate energy in <McItem slug="techreborn:diesel_generator" />.

## Usage

<McItem slug="techreborn:nitrodiesel_cell" /> is used to generate energy. 1 cell or bucket will generate 400k Energy in <McItem slug="techreborn:diesel_generator" />.
