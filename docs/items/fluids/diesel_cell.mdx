The **Diesel Cell** is a fluid cell added by Tech Reborn. It is used to produce energy in <McItem slug="diesel_generator" pack="techreborn" /> or for further processing into <McItem slug="nitrodiesel_cell" pack="techreborn" />.

<McItem slug="techreborn:diesel_cell" /> is used to generate energy. 1 cell or bucket will generate 128k Energy in <McItem slug="techreborn:diesel_generator" />.
