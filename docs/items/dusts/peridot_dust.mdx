## Usage

**Peridot Dust** could be further processed in <McItem slug="techreborn:industrial_electrolyzer" inline={true}/>:  
9x <McItem slug="techreborn:peridot_dust" inline={true}/> + <McItem slug="techreborn:empty_cell" inline={true}/> →  
2x <McItem slug="techreborn:magnesium_dust" inline={true}/> + 2x <McItem slug="techreborn:iron_dust" inline={true}/> + <McItem slug="techreborn:silicon_cell" inline={true}/> + 2x <McItem slug="techreborn:compressed_air_cell" inline={true}/>

4x <McItem slug="techreborn:peridot_dust" inline={true}/> + 16x <McItem slug="minecraft:TNT" inline={true}/> → 3x <McItem slug="techreborn:peridot" inline={true}/> + 12x <McItem slug="techreborn:dark_ashes" inline={true}/>
