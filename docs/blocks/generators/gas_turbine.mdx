# Gas Turbine

The **Gas Turbine** produces energy from various gases. It has an internal tank that can hold 10k mB of fluid worth which is consumed as it is converted to energy.

| Tier   | Generation Rate | Output  | Storage |
|--------|------------------|---------|---------|
| Medium | 16 E/t           | 128 E/t | 1 mE    |

### Fuel

| Fuel     | E/1000 mB |
|----------|-----------|
| Hydrogen | 15,000    |
| Methane  | 45,000    |

### Usage

- Place a cell or bucket of fuel into the input slot to fill the internal tank  
- Can also be filled by an external **[Tank](/docs/blocks/tanks/)**  
- Outputs energy on all sides
