The **Plasma Generator** produces energy from Heliumplasma. It has an internal tank that can hold 10k mB of fluid which is consumed as it is converted to energy.

| Tier    | Generation Rate | Output    | Storage |
|---------|------------------|-----------|---------|
| Extreme | 400 E/t          | 2048 E/t  | 500 mE  |

### Fuel

| Fuel         | E/1000 mB |
|--------------|-----------|
| Heliumplasma | 8,192,000 |

### Usage

- Place a cell or bucket of fuel into the input slot to fill the internal tank  
- Can also be filled by an external **[Tank](/docs/blocks/tanks/)**  
- Outputs energy on all sides
