# Plasma Generator

The **Plasma Generator** produces energy from Heliumplasma. It has an internal **[Tank](/img/mods/techreborn/basic_tank_unit)** that can hold 10k mB of fluid (10 **[Buckets](/mods/minecraft/bucket)** or **[Cells](/items/fluid/empty_cell)** worth) which is consumed as it is converted to energy.

| Tier    | Generation Rate | Output    | Storage |
|---------|------------------|-----------|---------|
| Extreme | 400 E/t          | 2048 E/t  | 500 mE  |

### Fuel

| Fuel         | E/1000 mB |
|--------------|-----------|
| Heliumplasma | 8,192,000 |

### Usage

- Place a cell or bucket of fuel into the input slot to fill the internal tank  
- Can also be filled by an external **[Tank](/img/mods/techreborn/basic_tank_unit)**  
- Outputs energy on all sides
