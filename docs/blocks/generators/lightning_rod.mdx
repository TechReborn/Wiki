# Lightning Rod

**Lightning Rod** is a **[passive generator](/energy/generators/passive_generators)** that produces energy from lightning strikes during [thunderstorms](http://minecraft.gamepedia.com/Thunderstorm). Attaching more than one **Iron Fence** on top of the machine will increase the chance of lightning.

| Tier    | Generation Rate | Output    | Storage |
|---------|------------------|-----------|---------|
| Extreme | 256 kE/bolt      | 2048 E/t  | 100 mE  |

### Usage

- Put **[Iron Fence](/img/mods/techreborn/iron_fence)** on top of the block. More fences will increase the chance of lightning striking the generator.  
- Outputs energy on all sides except the top
