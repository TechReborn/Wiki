# Wind Mill

**Wind Mill** is a **[passive generator](/docs/blocks/generators/passive_generators)** that produces energy from wind.

| Tier     | Generation Rate | Output   | Storage |
|----------|------------------|----------|---------|
| Advanced | 2–2.5 E/t¹       | 128 E/t  | 10 kE   |

¹ Generates +25% E during [thunderstorms](http://minecraft.gamepedia.com/Thunderstorm)

### Usage

- Must be placed above y=64 to produce energy  
- Outputs energy on all sides except the front
