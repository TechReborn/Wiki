# Diesel Generator

The **Diesel Generator** produces energy from various fluids. It has an internal tank that can hold 10k mB of fluid (10 **Buckets** or **Cells**) worth which is consumed as it is converted to energy.

| Tier | Fuel Type | Output | Storage |
|------|------------|--------|---------|
| Basic | Diesel | 32 E/t | 10 kE |

### Usage

- Place a cell or bucket of fuel into the input slot to fill the internal tank  
- Can also be filled by an external **[Tank](/docs/blocks/tanks/)**  
- Outputs energy on all sides
