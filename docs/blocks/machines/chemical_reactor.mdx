title: Chemical Reactor
---

<ItemImage file="chemical_reactor" alt="Chemical Reactor" size="200" />

The **Chemical Reactor** is used to combine two <McItem slug="techreborn:lava_cell" /> (and other assorted items) into a third fluid or item. It has input rate of 128 Energy and an internal buffer of 10,000 Energy.
