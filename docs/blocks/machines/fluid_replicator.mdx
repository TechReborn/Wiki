The **Fluid Replicator** is an advanced machine capable of replicating fluid placed inside the hollow of a multiblock structure using energy and <McItem slug="techreborn:UU-Matter" inline={true}/>. The **Fluid Replicator** is a high-tier machine with 1024 FE input rate and has an internal buffer of 1.6M FE.

## Building Instructions

The **Fluid Replicator** requires a 3×3×1 multiblock with a hollow center.

- **First layer**: 8x <McItem slug="techreborn:reinforced_machine_casing" inline={true}/> (leave the center block empty)
- Attach the machine controller to the multiblock on the same level.

<img src="/img/blocks/fluid_replicator.png" alt="Fluid Replicator" />

## Usage

Put the fluid to replicate inside the hollow in the multiblock.

Provide energy and <McItem slug="techreborn:UU-Matter" inline={true}/> to begin replication. You can use **buckets**, **cells**, or **pipes** to extract the replicated fluid from the internal tank.
