## Energy Storage Upgrade

The **Energy Storage Upgrade** is a [machine upgrade](/docs/blocks/machines/upgrades) that increases the amount of energy the machine can store.

It adds 40kE to the storage of the machine, and does stack with other upgrades of the same type.
