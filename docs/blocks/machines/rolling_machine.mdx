# Rolling Machine

The **Rolling Machine** is used to produce various coils, and vanilla items such as rails and carts.

| Tier | Input     | Power Draw | Storage  |
|------|-----------|------------|----------|
| Low  | 32 E/t    | 5 E/t      | 10 kE    |
