# Saw Mill

The **Saw Mill** is a multi-block wood processing machine.

## Building instructions

The **Saw Mill** multi-block has 3 layers:

- **First layer**: 9x <McItem slug="techreborn:basic_machine_casing" inline={true}/>  
  <img src="/img/blocks/saw_mill_layer1.png" alt="Layer 1"/>
- **Second layer**: 8x <McItem slug="techreborn:advanced_machine_casing" inline={true}/> with a central hole filled with water  
  <img src="/img/blocks/saw_mill_layer2.png" alt="Layer 2"/>
- **Third layer**: 9x <McItem slug="techreborn:basic_machine_casing" inline={true}/>  
  <img src="/img/blocks/saw_mill_layer3.png" alt="Layer 3"/>

Place the **Saw Mill** block on the top and connect it to a power source.  
<img src="/img/blocks/saw_mill.png" alt="Saw Mill"/>

## Usage

Put wood and water into the input slot. The **Saw Mill** will produce planks, and with a chance, some <McItem slug="techreborn:saw_dust" inline={true}/> and paper.
