Chemical Reactor

The Chemical Reactor is used to combine two Lava Cell (and other assorted items) into a third fluid or item. It has input rate of 128 Energy and an internal buffer of 10,000 Energy.

Recipe​

Crafting Table - Techreborn:chemical Reactor
